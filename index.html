About HelpLink Network

HelpLink Network is a student-powered support system created to simplify getting help or making money by offering your skills. Whether you need someone to type an assignment, design a flyer, or help with tutoring – we’ve got people ready to assist.

Our goal is to build a strong and trustworthy network where students collaborate, support one another, and grow financially and academically.

How It Works

  • Join the appropriate WhatsApp group (requesters or helpers).
  • Requesters submit task details and proposed budget.
  • Helpers pick tasks, negotiate if needed, and get approved by admin.
  • Admin connects requester and helper privately.
  • Payment is made and task is done with admin supervision.
